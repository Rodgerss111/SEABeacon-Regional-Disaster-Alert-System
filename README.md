# SEABeacon-Regional-Disaster-Alert-System
<p align="justify">
SEABeacon is a cross-border disaster intelligence platform that aggregates existing storm 
and flood forecast data from ASEAN national meteorological agencies — PAGASA, BMKG, TMD, 
MetMalaysia, and VNMHA — and uses AI to identify which forecasts carry cross-border implications 
for neighboring countries, then delivers localized early warnings through the social platforms citizens 
already use, before disaster arrives. 

<br> Three AI layers power the system. First, a cross-border impact classifier trained on historical 
typhoon tracks and flood events determines whether a forecast originating in one country poses 
significant risk to neighboring nations, and at what severity. Second, a natural language processing 
layer continuously monitors public meteorological agency feeds and regional news outlets across 
ASEAN in multiple languages — Filipino, Bahasa, Thai, Vietnamese, and Malay — aggregating 
cross-border risk signals faster than manual tracking. Third, an automated alert generation layer 
produces localized warnings in each country's language and distributes them through Facebook, 
WhatsApp, LINE, and Zalo simultaneously.
</p>
