Download these 4 files from your Kaggle Model and place them in this folder:

  1. best_model_phase2.keras
  2. scaler_params_v2.json
  3. feature_cols.json
  4. basin_meta.csv

Source (Kaggle):
  /kaggle/input/models/enzodacanay/newphase2model-asean/keras/default/1/

Without these files, model_loader.load_artifacts() will raise
FileNotFoundError when any script in this folder runs.
